Discussion on Olin's Sexual and Relationship Respect Support/and Resources

Ellie Funkhouser, Austin Greene, Victoria Preston

Austin Greene (R2), Victoria Preston (Honor Board Chair/R3), and Ellie Funkhouser (Peer Advocates co-leader) will be facilitating a discussion on Olin's support and resources for students around sexual respect and relationship health. What do we have now? How could we make it better? What really happens in a Title IX investigation? All this and more.

Talking Yellow - Film Screening + Discussion

James Wu, Doyung Lee, Ellie Funkhouser, Melisa Lopardo

Talking Yellow is a documentary about the experiences of Asian American men at Olin College. Its purpose is to prompt a conversation about how Olin's student culture deals with racial differences. We will be screening this 15-minute movie at 2PM and 4PM in the AC126 - all Oliners are encouraged to stop by.
