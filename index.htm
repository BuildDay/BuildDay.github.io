10AM - 5PM, Olin College

Projects

Brainstorm with BOW

Jessica McCarthy, Jean Huang

Join Jessica and Jean for a community brainstorming session to build the BOW Collaboration! Bring your ideas, help identify needs within the community and develop ideas to Build BOW! All are welcome at any time during the session; we will have tasty desserts and tea.

Path to Lot B

Andrew Deaver, Mary Martin, Katie Hite

Ever want to get a path down to Lot B and avoid all the mud? We will be building a path linking the inner circle road between East and West Hall with Lot B to enable better access to cars. Come join us!

Discussion on Diversity

James Wu, Doyung Lee, Ellie Funkhouser, Melisa Lopardo

Talking Yellow is a documentary about the racial experiences of Asian American men at Olin College. Its purpose is to prompt a conversation about the role of race and ethnicity in Olin’s student culture. We will be screening this 12-minute movie in the morning and afternoon on Build Day – all Oliners are encouraged to stop by.

Fixing Go Bikes

Anisha Nakagawa, Aaron Greiner, Nur Shlapobersky

Want to help make GO bikes better than ever? Join us on Build Day to help repair bikes and get the area in better shape, so that we can make the bikes more reliable and convenient.

Easy Welding

Janie Harari

We'll be offering an introduction to MIG and TIG welding in the machine shop on Build Day and letting anyone who is interested lay down some weld beads.

Origami

Evan Dorsky

Massive Modular Origami (round 3): Come fold paper modules to build awesome Origami sculptures with the rest of the Olin community! No experience necessary. We’ll also be finding places around campus to put our art.

SERV Kickball

SERV

Play SERV Kickball on from 2-3:30pm on the soccer fields! Sign up as an individual or as a team on this Google Doc and donate $20 per team to SERV. All proceeds will go to Kick It to cure children’s cancer!
